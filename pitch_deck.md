## Slide 2: The Problem
**Headline**: The Trust Gap in Indian Online Auctions
**Points**:
- **Fragmented Market**: Highly fragmented antique and collectibles market in India.
- **Trust Deficit**: Lack of verified sellers and secure bidding mechanisms.
- **Currency Friction**: Complicated international platforms that don't prioritize local INR transactions.
- **Data Loss**: Common issues with item record persistence on small platforms.

---

## Slide 3: The Solution
**Headline**: A Modern, Localized, and Robust Platform
**Points**:
- **Localized Experience**: Built specifically for the Indian market with ₹ INR at the core.
- **Immutable Persistence**: Leveraging Vercel Blob storage to ensure auction records and images are never lost.
- **Smart Bidding**: Automated increment handling and live status tracking.
- **Verified Community**: Role-based access for Buyers, Sellers, and Admins.
